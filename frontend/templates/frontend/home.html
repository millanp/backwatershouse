Welcome to the website

This is Malabar House, a beautiful newly built house on the Malabar Coast.
On this site, you will be able to browse the rooms, check out the house, and book a stay. This was built to help automate the process of lending rooms to friends and family, but note that the owner is still in charge!

The Rooms

Learn more about the rooms you may wish to reserve.

My Visits

A dashboard of all of your upcoming visits, where you can cancel bookings, view the approval status of room requests, and pay the non-family fee.

Book a Stay

Book as many rooms as you like (or even the whole house!) Includes a convenient calendar to view booked dates for different rooms. Your request will be sent to the owner of the house.
